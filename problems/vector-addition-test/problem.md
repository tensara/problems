Problem Statement
-----------------

Perform element-wise addition of two vectors: $$ c_i = a_i + b\_i $$

Input
-----

*   Vectors $a$ and $b$ of length $N$

Output
------

*   Vector $c$ of length $N$ containing the element-wise sum
